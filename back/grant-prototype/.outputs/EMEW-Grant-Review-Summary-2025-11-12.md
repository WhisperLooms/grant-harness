## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Key Risks Identified

#### Risk 1: Advisory Service Delay (IGP)
**Impact:** 8-14 week wait before grant eligibility
**Mitigation:** Start Advisory Service immediately while pursuing CSIRO Kick-Start in parallel

#### Risk 2: Co-Funding Requirements
**Impact:** All major grants require 30-50% matched funding
**Mitigation:**
- Secure investor commitments early
- Stage applications (smaller first, larger later)
- Use grant proceeds from smaller programs as co-funding for larger ones

#### Risk 3: Long Application Timelines
**Impact:** 4-9 months from application to funding
**Mitigation:**
- Run multiple applications in parallel
- Maintain cash flow during application period
- Use CSIRO ($10-50K) as bridge funding

#### Risk 4: Eligibility Thresholds
**Impact:** Some programs have specific requirements (e.g., emissions, location)
**Mitigation:**
- Thorough eligibility assessment before investing time
- Focus on programs with confirmed fit
- NSW HEI likely not applicable unless threshold met

#### Risk 5: Technology Readiness Level Alignment
**Impact:** Some programs require specific TRL stages
**Mitigation:**
- IGP Advisory Service helps identify best track (ESC vs. C&G)
- CSIRO Kick-Start validates technology and raises TRL
- Clear understanding of EMEW's current TRL critical

- CSIRO Kick-Start: 2-3 months
- IGP: 6-9 months
- ARP: 6-12 months

**Total Grant Preparation Investment Required:**
- Time: ~100-200 hours (spread over 3-6 months)
- Cash for co-funding: $5K-$50K for initial applications (scales with program size)
- Professional support: Consider grant consultant for major applications ($10-50K)

With EMEW's established technology, clear market need, and strong alignment with National Reconstruction Fund priorities (Value-add in Resources), the company is well-positioned to successfully secure multiple grants to accelerate growth and market expansion.
